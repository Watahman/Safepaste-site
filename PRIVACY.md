# SafePaste Privacy Policy

SafePaste processes pasted text, form values, and manually selected text only locally in the browser, and only after you paste, submit a form, or choose the context-menu action. Password fields are never read.

The extension stores locally: settings, domain rules, hashes of permitted non-secret values, and anonymous warning and anonymization counters. It does not store pasted text, form content, keys, tokens, passwords, or detection results.

SafePaste does not use analytics, cookies, browsing history, clipboard history, or remote code. ExtensionPay may communicate with its service for an optional Plus purchase; scanned content is never sent to it.
