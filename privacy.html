Privacy Policy

SafePaste is designed to help you avoid accidentally sharing sensitive information while keeping your content on your device.

In short: SafePaste scans locally in your browser. It does not collect or upload the text that it scans.

What SafePaste processes

SafePaste can process pasted text, text in submitted forms, and text you explicitly select to scan. This processing occurs locally in your browser. Password fields are not read.

What SafePaste stores

SafePaste stores its settings locally in the browser, including preferences, website rules, custom detection rules, hashes of permitted non-secret values, and anonymous warning or anonymization counters.

What SafePaste does not collect

  • Pasted text, form content, or selected text.
  • API keys, tokens, passwords, private keys, or payment details.
  • Detection results, browsing history, clipboard history, or analytics data.
  • Cookies or remote code.

Payments

Optional SafePaste Pro payments are handled by ExtensionPay and Stripe. ExtensionPay may process payment and account information needed to confirm paid access. SafePaste does not send scanned text, form values, secrets, or detection results to ExtensionPay.

Organization-managed devices

On managed devices, an organization may configure Chrome policies that determine which categories SafePaste blocks. These policies do not give the organization access to the text scanned by SafePaste.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when SafePaste changes. The latest version will always be published on this page.

Contact

For product questions, use the support link in the SafePaste Chrome Web Store listing or the SafePaste GitHub repository. Do not share secrets or personal data in public messages.
